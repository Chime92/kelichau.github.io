The Happy Basil
"The Happy Basil" is a co-op food blog run by Max and I, where we enjoy sharing our ideas with anything related to food. We have been dating since 2007 and began to learn to cook and bake together as a hobby that we both shared. We grew up with influences from our families' kitchens as well as our combination of Asian and American culture. We hope that our blog can bring you smiles, one bite at a time, from our kitchen to yours. Click here to redirect to "The Happy Basil".

Chicken in a Turtle Shell
"Chicken in a Turtle Shell" was founded in 2014 as a place where I can share my ideas on various topics such as lifestyle, fitness, and anything else that comes to mind. The title was created based on my zodiac sign as the rooster. The chicken being in a turtle shell is a metaphor of me finally coming out of my private shell to share who I am and what I stand for. The theme of this blog is "learning to be comfortable with being uncomfortable". I hope my posts can help you understand what life has to offer, make meaning of what time you have on this planet, and to inspire you to always put yourself out of your comfort zone.

About
Hi, I'm Keli Chau
As a...

developer, my goal is to try to come up with web/mobile app ideas that can be meaningful, helpful, and convenient for users.

creator, I aim to produce art that tell stories and inspire others to learn and master self-expression.

lifelong learner, I would like to motivate others to constantly push themselves out of their comfort zone.

human, I try to find ways to give back to the community and share my experiences in hopes of influencing people to develop their own set of principles that'll help them navigate through life on this earth.

This website is where you can find examples of my work as a web developer, photographer/videographer, and graphic designer. You'll also see sprinkles of my journey as a human, lifelong learner, and artist with content that is hopefully informational, inspirational, and insightful for readers and viewers alike.

Browse my GitHub to see what I'm currently developing, subscribe to my YouTube channel for video content, visit my SoundCloud for music covers, check out my Flickr and Facebook for more visuals of my projects, and subscribe to my newsletter here.

If you would like to grab a cup of (bubble) tea or have an online meeting to discuss your next project, feel free to call me at 770.904.1680 or email me. I am available Monday through Saturday between 7:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. EST.

About



Human. Lifelong Learner. Artist.

Hello. My name is Keli Chau and I'm a human, lifelong learner, artist, photographer, and filmmaker from Atlanta, Georgia. When I'm not shooting, I develop websites and dedicate my time to helping small businesses and artists grow by providing media services that include photography, web development, videography, print, graphic design, and social media management. I believe that one should appreciate all aspects of life because there is uncertainty on where humans end up when we pass. With this belief, I am passionate about learning anything I'm interested in on this earth, but above all, I prioritize family.

Thank you for stopping by my website. You can browse through my galleries, lifestyle blog, designs, projects, and services while you're here. If you would like to touch base for a collaboration, services, or to simply say hello, please feel free to contact me and connect with me on social media.

Cheers,

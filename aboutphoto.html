Kelly Chau Photography














History

Started in the comfort of her own home, Kelly Chau has been working with photography since 2004. Having launched her own freelance business 10 years later, "Kelly Chau Photography" strives to provide their clients with the utmost customer service and professionalism, while delivering quality-worthy photos. We believe photography taken at the right moment will capture the true essence of the subject. We work to achieve our goals while also meeting our clients' requests. Our work consists or bright and vibrant colors while focused on capturing our clients in their natural state. We hope our photos will leave impressions of appreciating the simple joys in life.

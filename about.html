About


Artist & Lifelong Learner

    Kelly Chau is an artist based in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. At a young age, she showed interest in multiple creative platforms that helped her with self-expression. Other than her passion for the arts, she places high importance on mental and physical health, emphasizing on prevention over curation.

    Her number one principle is honesty—to others and to oneself. She lives by her motto, "Learning to be comfortable with being uncomfortable," and strongly believes that one should always create their own path in life rather than waiting for it to be paved for them. When she's not working on improving herself, she finds joy in the simple things that life has to offer.

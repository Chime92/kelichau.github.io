About


Artist & Lifelong Learner

Kelly Chau is a photographer based in Atlanta, Georgia. At a young age, she showed interest in multiple mediums of art that helped her with self-expression. Other than her passion for the arts, she places high importance on mental and physical health and often shares her ideas on prevention over curation.

Her number one principle is honesty—to others and to oneself. She lives by her motto, "Learning to be comfortable with being uncomfortable," and strongly believes that one should always create their own path in life rather than waiting for it to be paved for them. She hopes that she will be able to inspire others to follow their passion and continuously live their life out of the comfort zone.

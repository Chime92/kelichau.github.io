About





Artist & Lifelong Learner


I have been intrigued with photography since age eleven when I realized how amazing it is that photographs can evoke different kinds of emotion in a person. Since then, I have been experimenting with different techniques and styles over the years to feed my curiosity and passion in this craft.

Besides freelancing, I'm currently at Georgia Gwinnett College (GGC) working on my B.S. in psychology. Other than studying full-time there, I also work as an Assistant Photographer with the Office of Public Affairs. I organize files on our college's photography page as well as assist in set up and photographing various events and everyday life on campus.

"Kelly Chau Photography" strives to provide our clients with professionalism, quality, and creativity, in hopes of leaving our clients to appreciating the simple joys in life. You can read more of our core values here.

I believe that life's too short to live a life filled with comfort. I encourage each of you to embrace who you are, share your contributions, and spread your love to the world.

Thank you for stopping by. I hope you enjoy your visit and find what you are looking for. If you have any questions or comments, please do not hesitate in contacting us.

About


Hello and welcome to my little space on the internet. My name is Keliana Chau and I was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. I consider myself a human, lifelong learner, and artist, all in that order. My creative side wasn't shared with others until 2014 when I decided to launch a local freelance photography service while finishing up my bachelor's degree. Minor but precious opportunities were given to me when I decided to express myself and it has brought me memories that I will forever keep in mind.

Besides photography, I also enjoy singing, writing, and basically any creative outlets that I can put my hands on. I also have a small interest in web designing, which led me to coding this website that you're on. When I'm not working on improving my craft, I find joy in spending time with loved ones, baking, traveling, and trying new things.

About



Human. Lifelong Learner. Artist.
Hello and welcome to my little space on the world wide web. My name is Keli Chau and I am a human, lifelong learner, artist, and creative consultant for Keli Chau Media, creating original content and providing the necessary tools and services for individuals, local, and small business owners in metro Atlanta to launch and maintain their online presence.

I enjoy immersing myself in culture, nature, and creativity through constant learning, travel, photography, writing, filmmaking, and web design. An avid supporter of minimalism, I believe appreciating the simple things life has to offer provides a humbling bliss that cannot be found from materialistic ideals and what is important is to be understanding, kind, generous, and willing to learn throughout life. When I'm not working on myself, you can find me spending time with family, soaking in nature, or reading a book.

Thank you for stopping by, you can browse through my gallery, blog, projects, and services and rates. If you would like to touch base for a collaboration, services, or to simply say hello, please feel free to contact me and connect with me on social media.

Cheers,

About

Human. Lifelong Learner. Artist.
Kelly Chau is a lifelong student, environment and animal advocate, and photography, music, and film aficionado.

At a young age, she showed interest in multiple creative platforms that helped her with self-expression. Aside from her passion for the arts, she is also an advocate for the environment and animals, and is continuously learning ways to contribute.

Her number one principle is honesty—to others and to oneself. She places high importance on mental and physical health, emphasizing on prevention over curation, and believes that one should always create their own path in life rather than waiting for it to be paved for them. When she's not working on improving herself, she finds joy in the simple things that life has to offer.

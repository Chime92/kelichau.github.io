    Official Website Launched
    Saturday, May 30, 2015

    Welcome to my website. I am happy to finally be able to share the launch of this site with you. Thank you for supporting and keeping up with this incredible learning experience in this thing that we call life.

    The launch of this site may not seem much, but it is something I am proud of because less than a year ago, I told myself I wanted to officially take up coding and create my own website from scratch.

    Something else that you may not know about me is that I have been dabbling with html when I started blogging on Xanga at age eleven. I continued to do so throughout these past years with various blogging platforms, such as LiveJournal, Blogger, and WordPress, to name a few. That experience helped me process everything faster when I decided to take a web design course as one of my electives last spring. I can't take all the credit though---I received additional help from a friend of mine and from fellow tech-enthusiasts that are so generous to share their codes, ideas, and tips on the world wide web. With all the above, I was able to pull this off.

    This place is where I will be able share all the projects I'm working on---Photography, music, blogging, and more. If you were reading the 'Chicken in a Turtle Shell' blog, all the posts from there is now posted on here. You can keep updated on new posts by subscribing to the mailing list.

    Hopefully you are able to navigate through this site with ease and that you will get to know me better from my projects. I wish that my experiences will inspire you to learn to be comfortable with being uncomfortable.

    Take a look around and let me know what you think of it. Don't be too harsh on me though, it is my first website, and I still have a lot to learn. Not just in coding, but also in life.

    Cheers,
    Kelly

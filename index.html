    Official Website Launched
    Saturday, May 30, 2015

    Welcome to my official website. I am happy to finally be able to share the launch of this site with you. Thank you for supporting and keeping up with this incredible learning experience in this thing that we call life.

    The launch of this site may not seem much, but it is something I am excited about because less than a year ago, I told myself I wanted to officially take up coding and create my own website from scratch. With some guidance from a friend of mine and tech-enthusiasts who are so generous to share their ideas, tips, and codes on the world wide web, I was able to pull this off.

    On here, you will be able to find the projects that I have done as well as current ones—photography, music, blogging, and more. You can stay updated on new posts and updates by subscribing to the mailing list on the 'Contact' page and through the various social media platforms that I am on.

    I wish that my experiences will inspire you to learn to be comfortable with being uncomfortable. Take a look around and let me know what you think of it. Don't be too harsh though, it is my first time designing and coding an entire website.

    Cheers,
    Kelly

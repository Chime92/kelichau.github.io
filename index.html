October Updates
Friday, October 23, 2015

Y'all! It has been too long. How has everyone been? It's my last year of undergrad (yes!) so I have been busy with classes and working the past couple of months. Since it's been a while, I wanted to share some updates with you.

There are a couple of minor changes with the website. I am no longer blogging for "Chicken in a Turtle Shell". Instead, I have decided to put my effort into "It's Chau Time", a collaborative food blog. For those who has been staying updated with my fitness journey and has questions about fitness tips, etc., feel free to contact me via email or social media and I will try my best to answer them.

I became a blog contributor for Kollaboration not too long ago, and have been learning a lot from it. For those who do not know, Kollaboration is a non-profit organization with a platform to help promote diversity, inspiring Asian and Pacific Islanders to pursue careers in the arts.

I have done a couple of recap posts for ABC's newest T.V. show, "Dr. Ken". I will be writing a post soon on Asian-Americans and their struggle in choosing between passion and obligation. Be on the lookout for that here.

That's all the updates for now. Let me know if you have any questions and remember to live your life with as much discomfort as you can!

Cheers,
Kelly

Website Launched
Saturday, May 30, 2015

Welcome to my website! I am excited to finally be able to share the launch of this site with you. Thank you for your support in keeping up with this incredible learning experience in this thing that we call life.

The launch of this site might seem minor, but it's something exciting to me because less than a year ago, I told myself to stop dabbling and officially take up coding in order to create a website from scratch. It's nothing fancy-smancy, but with some guidance from a friend and tech-enthusiasts who are generous with sharing their ideas on the WWW, I was able to pull this off.

On here you will be able to find projects from my past and current ones: photography, music, blogging, and more. You can stay updated by subscribing to the mailing list on the "Contact" page or through various social media platforms I'm on.

I wish my experiences will inspire you to learn to be comfortable with being uncomfortable! Take a look around and feel free to let me know what your thoughts are.

Cheers,
Kelly
